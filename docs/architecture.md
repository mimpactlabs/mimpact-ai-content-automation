# Mimpact AI Content Automation

## Vision

Build an AI-powered platform that automates the complete content production workflow—from content collection, AI analysis, clip generation, review, and publishing.

---

## MVP Goal

Input:

- YouTube URL

Output:

- 1 Short Video

---

## High Level Workflow

YouTube URL

↓

Download Video

↓

Speech To Text

↓

AI Analysis

↓

Find Best Clip

↓

Generate Subtitle

↓

Render Video

↓

Manual Review

↓

Publish

---

- Modular
- Scalable
